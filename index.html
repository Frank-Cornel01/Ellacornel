Overview

This training plan is designed to provide comprehensive instruction and practice in 3d design for both residential and commercial spaces over the course of one month. The plan consits of four sessions, with two classes per week, where the first class is for teaching and the second class is for practicals. The aim is to equip learners with the skills and knowledge needed to create detailed and visually appealing residential and commercial designs using 3D software(Cohom).
